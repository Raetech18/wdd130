Main Salmon River

Explore the stunning beauty of the Main Salmon River, known as the "River of No Return." This 6-day adventure takes you through the heart of Idaho's remote and rugged wilderness, where you'll experience thrilling rapids, stunning scenery, and unforgettable memories.

Rogue River

Embark on a 4-day journey down the Rogue River in Oregon, where you'll encounter a perfect blend of adrenaline-pumping rapids and serene, picturesque landscapes. Discover hidden waterfalls, ancient rock formations, and diverse wildlife along the way.

Futaleufu River

Venture to the remote and stunning Futaleufu River in Chile for an unforgettable 8-day rafting expedition. Tackle world-class rapids, explore pristine glaciers, and immerse yourself in the rich cultural traditions of the local Patagonian communities.
