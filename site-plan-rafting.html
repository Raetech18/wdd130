Purpose

As the owner of a whitewater rafting business, my purpose of establishing an online site is to effectively market and promote my rafting business, and also prove detailed and up-to-date information about the rafting trips my company offers.

Audience

Our water rafting website aims to cater to adventure enthusiasts and outdoor lovers who are seeking thrilling experiences on the water. Our target audience includes individuals of various age groups and fitness levels who are interested in whitewater rafting as a recreational activity. Are you a beginner?, or a family with young children who want to experience safe and enjoyable whitewater rafting adventures?, or an experienced rafter looking for challenging rapids and adrenaline-pumping adventures?
